## longer prison sentences vs better ways

I believe there are better ways to reduce crime. First and foremost, longer prison sentences are a way to use fear to scare people. Although it is useful to some extent, people will not commit crimes because it won't create any benefits. For instance, there were large number of thieves in China about 30 years ago. They are ubiquitous around train stations, bus stations, even on the trains due to high population. At that time, stealing high-value iterms such as jewelry, cell phone, wallet, etc. made them rich. They had ways to sell. However, we merely see any thieves in China now, thanks to the development of digital currency. There is no money in wallets. Cellphones are locked. Even apps are locked. Stealing is worthless compared with 30 years ago. Crime has reduced because of technology has developed. Sentences are barely changed.
